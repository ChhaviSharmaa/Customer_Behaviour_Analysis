# 🛒 Customer Behaviour Analysis: End-to-End Business Intelligence

## 📌 Project Overview
This project performs a comprehensive analysis of consumer shopping trends to identify growth opportunities and optimize marketing strategies. By integrating **Python** for data cleaning, **MySQL** for relational querying, and **Power BI** for visualization, I transformed raw transactional data into actionable business recommendations.

## 🚀 Workflow & Methodology

### 1. Data Cleaning (`Python`)
* Handled **missing values** and standardized column names using `snake_case`.
* Engineered new features like `age_group` to better segment the customer base.
* Exported the cleaned dataset into a structured `.csv` for database injection.

### 2. Relational Database Querying (`MySQL`)
Executed **10+ complex business queries** to extract critical insights:
* **Revenue Analysis:** Identified that Male customers contribute **$157,890** in total revenue.
* **Customer Segmentation:** Classified **3,116 "Loyal" customers** based on purchase frequency.
* **Opportunity Identification:** Isolated **2,518 repeat buyers** who are currently not subscribed.

### 3. Data Visualization (`Power BI`)
* Developed an **interactive dashboard** to track Key Performance Indicators (KPIs).
* Visualized demographic trends and product performance to support executive decision-making.

## 📈 Key Insights
* **The Power Shopper Niche:** Identified **839 high-spending customers** who utilize discounts but maintain above-average transaction values.
* **Top Performers:** `Jewelry`, `Blouses`, and `Sandals` were identified as the highest revenue-generating products.
* **Demographic Lead:** The **Young Adult** age group emerged as the most profitable segment, contributing **$62,143** in sales.

---

## 💡 Strategic Recommendations
* **Subscription Push:** Target the **2,518 non-subscribed repeat buyers** with a "loyalty-first" conversion campaign.
* **Margin Protection:** Optimize discount policies for high-sensitivity items like `Hats` and `Sneakers` to protect profit margins.
* **Marketing Focus:** Reallocate budget toward the **Young Adult** demographic and **Express Shipping** users for a higher **ROAS** (Return on Ad Spend).
